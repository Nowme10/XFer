KYC means "Know Your Customer." It is a due diligence process we use as a requirement for banks to verify customer identity and assess and monitor customer risk. These processes are required by the Bank Secrecy Act of 1970 and further expanded under the U.S Patriot Act. The information you provide is secure and will not be shared or sold to third parties as per our privacy policy
